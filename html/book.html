About the Book

"The Alchemist" is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho. It tells the mystical story of Santiago, a shepherd boy who dreams of traveling in search of a worldly treasure as extravagant as any ever found. The book is an inspiring tale about following your dreams and listening to your heart.
